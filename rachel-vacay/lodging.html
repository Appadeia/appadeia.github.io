Greenland's Best Hotels

Hotel Hans Egede

star star star star star_border attach_money attach_money attach_money

A 3-minute walk from Hans Egede Church, this modern hotel in an industrial-chic building is 1 kilometer from the Greenland National Museum.
Relaxed rooms come with free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and tea and coffeemakers. Upgraded rooms feature sitting areas, and city views; some have minibars. Suites add living rooms, and contemporary apartments come with kitchenettes. Minimum-stay rules apply to apartments.
A breakfast buffet is included (fee for apartment guests), as is parking. There's also a fitness center, as well as 2 elegant restaurants, and a sleek piano bar.

Hotel Arctic

star star star star star_border attach_money attach_money attach_money

This modern hotel lies 2 km from Ilulissat Museum's displays about polar explorer Knud Rassmusen and 4 km from Ilulisaat Icefjord.
Featuring local art, the relaxed rooms have cable TV and Wi-Fi (fee). Most also offer iceberg views and minibars, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms add sitting areas. In the summer, igloo-style cabins are available.
Freebies include breakfast and shuttle service to the airport, harbor and town. Other amenities include a guest computer, a cafe and a conference center, as well as a Nordic restaurant featuring locally sourced ingredients.

Hotel Icefiord

star star star star star_half attach_money attach_money

Overlooking icebergs in Disko Bay, this scenic hotel is 2 km from the Ilulissat town center and 3 km from Knud Rasmussen's Museum, dedicated to the famed Danish explorer.
Featuring pared-back decor, the simple rooms and apartments have flat-screen TVs and Wi-Fi (fee). Upgraded rooms add sofas and some have balconies, while family rooms have kitchenettes. Airy apartments offer kitchens.
Free breakfast is served in a bright dining area. Other amenities include a hip bar, meeting rooms and a lounge, as well as a terrace with views of the bay.

Hotel Nuuk Seamen's Home

star star star star star_border attach_money attach_money

Atop a craggy hill, this humble hotel is 1.6 km from the Katuaq cultural center, 2.1 km from Nuuk Cathedral and 2.4 km from the Greenland National Museum.
The modest rooms with shared bathrooms offer TVs and free Wi-Fi. Upgraded rooms add en suite facilities. Some rooms have water or mountain views.
A breakfast buffet is served in the cafeteria. Other amenities include conference facilities, an Internet cafe and a living room with magazines and games. There's also a guest laundry and a guest freezer.

Hotel Angmagssalik

star star star star star_half attach_money

Set on a hill overlooking the town of Tasiilaq, this warm hotel with an adjacent annex also offers views of King Oscar’s Harbour and the surrounding mountains. It’s a 7-minute walk from the Ammassalik Museum and 1 km from the Tasiilaq Heliport.
Relaxed standard rooms come with en suite bathrooms, TVs and Wi-Fi access (surcharge). The annex has basic rooms with bunk beds and shared facilities.
Breakfast is free. Other amenities include a casual dining room, a coffee bar and a rustic lounge with a fireplace. There’s also a terrace, event space and a souvenir shop. The hotel offers boat, walking and helicopter tours (fees).

Hotel Kulusuk

star star star star star_half attach_money

The hotel is within a few minutes drive from the gate to East Greenland, Kulusuk Airport. At Greenland's only settlement hotel you can experience the true atmosphere of the unique lifestyle in an Inuit hunter settlement. The hotel has 34 room all including private facilities, rooms with mountain and seaview. An a very spacious dining an relaxing area.
